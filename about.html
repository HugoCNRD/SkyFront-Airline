Who we are

SkyFront Airlines Virtual is a Virtual airline created by Arendiuz during the summer of 2026. SkyFront recreates Frontier real world airline operations.
Our main hub is Denver, Colorado KDEN and we propose over 120 destinations, mainly in the US, but also Mexico, the Carribean and central America. We only operate airbus planes, flying all the A320 family from the A318 to the A321 (and soon A321 NEO !), with unique liveries representing the US wildlife !

Our vision

We think it is important to create a realistic enjoyable flight experience for everyone, and that is why we are dedicated to provide quality operations. Our goal is to remain as active as possible, to provide pilots a new VA experience. We alos want to create a community for each of our pilots, where everyon is welcomed !

Our Mission

Our goal is to provide the best virtual airline experience by:

• Offering multiple routes across all the United States, flying gorgeous Frontier's liveries
• Maintaining the VA active, with events, group flights, and community involvement
• Creating a welcoming community for all pilots
• Keeping a high level of realism with the VA.

We aim to create the best flying experience for all pilots
