Rank System

Our ranks are based on a simple, 4-ranks system, which will grant you access to more and more of our fleet as you file PIREPs and accumulta flight time !
Our ROTWs program and events are free to everyone !
We also created a ranking systems for those of you who control the skies as IFATCs !

Cadet - 0h -> 5h

This is the first rank you get after joining the VA.
Your first flight can be any flight from our database - codeshare included !
After your first flight, you will be allowed to fly the CRJ-700

Second Officer - 5h -> 20h

After exploring the vast CRJ-700 network with your first rank, you'll then be authorized to fly as well the A318-200 and A320-200 CEO and NEO (once available on the game)

First officer 20h -> 50 h

Congratulations ! You just hit 50h !
This rank will grant you access to A321-200 CEO and NEO (when available)

Captain 150+h h

WOW ! You have achieved the highest rank possible !
You'll have access to ALL codeshares (not only those selected each week in the ROTWs) and some extra perks, such as priority for take off during group flights/events
